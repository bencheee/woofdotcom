Tips for rehoming your dog

We understand how difficult the decision can be to give up a dog. If you are struggling, and are at risk of having to surrender, we may be able to help. There are many people looking for a new dog just like yours. Before considering surrendering your dog to a shelter, see if you can find another family who may be interested in giving your dog a new home.

Caution
Use caution when considering unknown individuals or families as your dog's new owners. Hold the initial meeting in a public place and ask questions to screen potential adopters. Share your expectations for your dog's new home. When you find a family that meets your and your dog's needs, ask for identification and contact information to follow up on how your dog is doing in its new home.
Time
Give yourself time to re-home your dog. It can often take weeks or even months to find a new home.
